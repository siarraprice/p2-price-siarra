The Camp Rainbow Mission is...

To provide free camping experiences to children undergoing treatment for, and survivors of, cancer and blood-related diseases and disorders. Through recreational and nurturing environments, campers and their families are provided with space to create positive memories, build self-esteem, make new friends and experience relief from the everyday stresses caused by their illness.
